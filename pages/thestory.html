Whats Our Story?

Who are we? Where are we going and where have we been?

Bunch of mad yokes

Our members come from all walks of life, but all affected by the housing crisis: evictions, massive rents, low standards, homelesnness, precarirty --- we've had it up to hear and we're not gonna take it anymore!

Taking a journey

Organising ourselves, our friends and communities to take power back and bring the fight to exploiting landlords, neoliberal government, security and homeless industries

Reach for the stars and hitting the moon

We want to make housing a human right in our country for all: with will keep fighting and growing till that is so
